About the Game

NoobsAreComing is a high-octane pixel arcade game. Defend your base against relentless waves of 'noobs' in this thrilling survival challenge. Master quick reflexes and strategic thinking to survive endless onslaughts.

Experience nostalgic pixel art and an immersive chiptune soundtrack. Each wave brings new enemy types, demanding quick thinking and precise aiming. How long can you last?

How to Play

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys.
  • Aim & Shoot: Mouse. Click to fire.
  • Survive: Eliminate all 'noobs' before they breach your defenses.
  • Strategize: Learn patterns and prioritize targets.
